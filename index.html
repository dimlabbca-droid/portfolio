About

Profile &
Objective

I work on interior spaces where every material junction, lighting choice and finishing tolerance matters, even when the final result is meant to feel effortless.

My career took me from hands-on site work to leading complex multi-stakeholder projects. I started close to the craft, through workshop and installation experience, before moving into technical drawing and full project management. At Wider SA in Montreux, I ran several luxury retail fit-outs simultaneously, coordinating both internal teams and networks of 10 or more external contractors per project. I reported on budget progress and key indicators to management, across budgets ranging from $800K to $1.6M.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Construction Project Management from IAE Lyon, backed by solid hands-on experience in millwork, interior fit-out and production detailing. I am currently strengthening my English and gaining exposure to North American professional culture, while actively looking for my next opportunity in Europe, particularly France or Switzerland.

Current objective. My goal is to contribute to high-end interior, retail or workplace projects where technical precision, coordination and execution quality truly matter.
